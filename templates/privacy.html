Last updated: April 25, 2026

1. Data We Collect

When you create an account, we collect basic information such as your name and email address. As you use Spendly, we store the financial data you choose to log — expense amounts, categories, dates, and descriptions. We also collect minimal technical data like your browser type and session activity to keep the Service running smoothly.

2. How We Use Your Data

Your data is used solely to provide and improve the Service: showing your expenses, generating summaries, and helping you understand your spending patterns. We do not use your financial data for advertising, and we never sell or rent your information to third parties. Aggregated, anonymized usage statistics may be used internally to improve features.

3. Data Storage

Your data is stored securely on managed servers with industry-standard encryption in transit and at rest. We retain your information for as long as your account is active. You may export or permanently delete your data at any time from your account settings — once deleted, it cannot be recovered.

4. Third Party Services

Spendly relies on a small number of trusted third-party providers for hosting, authentication, and email delivery. These providers process data only on our behalf and under strict confidentiality agreements. We do not embed advertising trackers or social media pixels in the Service.

5. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, want to request a copy of your data, or wish to delete your account, please reach out to us at privacy@spendly.example. We aim to respond to all privacy-related inquiries within seven business days.
